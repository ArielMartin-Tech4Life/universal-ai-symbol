# License Rationale

## Universal AI Symbol Project

**Version:** 1.0

---

# Why CC0?

The Universal AI Symbol project uses the Creative Commons CC0 1.0 Universal dedication.

The purpose is to remove unnecessary barriers between the symbol and the people who may find it useful.

---

# Goals

CC0 supports the project's principles:

* openness
* accessibility
* reuse
* modification
* distribution
* voluntary adoption

---

# No Ownership Model

The project does not intend to create exclusive ownership over the symbol.

The purpose is not control, but availability.

---

# Commercial and Non-Commercial Use

The symbol may be used by individuals, educators, researchers, artists, organizations, and companies.

No permission is required.

---

# Preservation of History

Although the symbol is openly available, the repository preserves the historical development of the proposal.

Future modifications can be documented while maintaining the original record.

---

# Principle

Open ideas grow through participation.

CC0 provides the foundation for that possibility.
