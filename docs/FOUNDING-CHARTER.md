# Founding Charter

## Universal AI Symbol Project

**Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Approved

**Created:** 2026

---

# Introduction

Throughout history, humanity has created symbols to communicate ideas that words alone cannot fully express.

Some symbols represent emotions.

Some represent values.

Some represent discoveries.

Others become part of everyday life simply because people find them useful.

Artificial Intelligence has become one of the defining technologies of our time, yet there is still no simple, open, and neutral symbol that represents it independently of any particular company, product, or technology.

This project exists as an open proposal to explore that possibility.

---

# Our Purpose

The purpose of this project is not to create ownership.

It is not to create exclusivity.

It is not to establish authority.

Its purpose is to offer an open symbol that anyone may freely adopt if they consider it useful.

Whether the symbol becomes widely recognized is not a decision that can be made by this project.

Only people, through voluntary use, can give meaning to a symbol.

---

# Our Principles

The project is guided by the following principles.

## Openness

The symbol belongs to everyone.

No permission should ever be required to use it.

No exclusive ownership is intended.

No artificial barriers should prevent its adoption.

---

## Neutrality

The symbol does not represent a company.

It does not represent a product.

It does not represent a government.

It does not represent a particular AI model.

It represents only the broader concept of Artificial Intelligence.

---

## Simplicity

The symbol should remain simple enough to be remembered, reproduced, and understood across cultures, languages, and technologies.

Its strength should come from clarity rather than complexity.

---

## Curiosity

Every form of intelligence begins with a question.

Curiosity is the origin of learning, discovery, science, creativity, and progress.

This project recognizes curiosity as one of its deepest inspirations.

---

## Humanity

Artificial Intelligence exists because humanity chose to create it.

The purpose of this project is therefore not only to represent AI, but also to recognize the relationship between human curiosity and artificial intelligence.

The symbol is intended to serve as a bridge rather than a boundary.

---

## Freedom

The symbol is released without restrictions under the CC0 dedication.

Individuals, educators, researchers, artists, developers, organizations, companies, and communities are free to use, modify, reproduce, distribute, commercialize, or build upon it without requesting permission.

---

# What We Will Not Do

This project will never claim to define an official global symbol.

It will never attempt to replace existing logos, brands, or trademarks.

It will never require attribution as a condition for use.

It will never restrict commercial or non-commercial adoption.

It will never discriminate against individuals or organizations wishing to use the symbol.

---

# Governance Philosophy

Ideas are stronger than ownership.

The long-term success of this project should depend on transparency, documentation, thoughtful discussion, and voluntary collaboration rather than centralized control.

Future contributors are encouraged to improve the project while respecting its founding principles.

---

# Looking Forward

The symbol itself is only one part of this project.

Equally important are the ideas it represents.

If future generations find this proposal useful, they are invited to continue developing it with the same openness, humility, and respect that inspired its beginning.

---

# Closing Statement

No symbol becomes universal because someone declares it so.

Symbols become universal because people choose to use them.

This project offers a symbol.

The world will decide whether it becomes part of its language.
