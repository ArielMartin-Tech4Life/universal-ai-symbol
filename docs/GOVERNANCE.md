# Governance

## Universal AI Symbol Project

**Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Approved

**Created:** 2026

---

# Purpose

This document describes the governance philosophy of the Universal AI Symbol project.

The project is designed as an open documentation initiative rather than a centralized authority.

---

# Principles

## Openness

The project welcomes discussion, improvements, translations, and alternative proposals.

---

## Transparency

Important decisions, changes, and historical developments should remain publicly documented.

---

## Voluntary Adoption

No individual or organization can declare a symbol universal.

Recognition depends on voluntary use by people and communities.

---

# Project Changes

Documentation improvements, translations, and technical corrections may be proposed through normal contribution channels.

Major changes affecting the symbol proposal should be documented separately.

---

# Symbol Proposals

Future alternatives should:

* explain their motivation
* document design decisions
* respect project principles
* remain open for discussion

---

# Ownership

The project does not seek exclusive control over the symbol.

Materials are released under CC0 to encourage unrestricted use.

---

# Final Principle

The project provides an open proposal.

Its future meaning is determined by those who choose to use it.
