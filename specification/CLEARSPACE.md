# Purpose

This document defines the clear space requirements for the Universal AI Symbol.

The purpose of clear space is to preserve the symbol's visibility, recognition, and visual independence when placed near other elements.

The symbol should always have enough surrounding space to remain clear and identifiable.

---

# Definition

Clear space is the minimum empty area surrounding the symbol where no text, graphics, borders, or other visual elements should interfere.

This protected area allows the symbol to maintain its visual identity across different applications.

---

# Clear Space Principle

The Universal AI Symbol should always be presented with sufficient breathing room.

The surrounding space should protect:

* recognition
* balance
* simplicity
* visual clarity

The symbol should never appear visually crowded.

---

---

# Minimum Protection Area

The clear space area should remain free from:

* text
* logos
* icons
* decorative elements
* borders
* competing visual objects

The symbol should not share its protected area with unrelated elements.

---

# Examples of Incorrect Use

Avoid:

* placing text directly against the symbol
* placing other logos too close
* reducing the clear space for aesthetic reasons
* creating visual competition around the symbol

---

# Applications

Clear space should be respected in:

* digital interfaces
* printed materials
* presentations
* educational materials
* signage
* physical products
* three-dimensional applications

# Principle

Clear space does not define the meaning of the symbol.

It protects the conditions that allow the symbol to be recognized.
