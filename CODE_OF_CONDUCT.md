# Code of Conduct

## Universal AI Symbol Project

**Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Approved

**Created:** 2026

---

# Purpose

The Universal AI Symbol project is built around openness, collaboration, and respect.

This Code of Conduct establishes the expected behavior for everyone participating in the project, including contributors, reviewers, community members, and anyone engaging with project discussions.

The goal is to maintain a constructive environment where ideas can be explored openly.

---

# Our Principles

## Respect

All participants should communicate with respect.

Different opinions, interpretations, cultures, backgrounds, and perspectives should be considered with openness.

---

## Constructive Discussion

Disagreements are expected in creative and collaborative projects.

Criticism should focus on ideas, proposals, designs, and documentation rather than individuals.

---

## Openness

The project welcomes contributions from people with different experiences and perspectives.

Participation should remain accessible and inclusive.

---

## Neutrality

Discussions should respect the project's independence from:

* companies
* products
* governments
* political organizations
* specific AI systems

The symbol represents the broader concept of Artificial Intelligence.

---

# Expected Behavior

Participants are encouraged to:

* communicate clearly and respectfully
* provide constructive feedback
* explain reasoning behind suggestions
* welcome questions from newcomers
* preserve the project's open philosophy

---

# Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable:

* harassment
* personal attacks
* discrimination
* threats
* deliberate disruption of discussions
* attempts to claim exclusive ownership of the project
* attempts to restrict lawful use of the symbol

---

# Handling Problems

If a community member experiences or observes behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, they are encouraged to report it through the project's official communication channels when available.

Reports should include:

* description of the issue
* relevant context
* links or references when applicable

Reports should be handled with discretion and fairness.

---

# Enforcement

Project maintainers may take appropriate action when necessary to preserve a respectful and productive environment.

Possible actions may include:

* requesting changes in behavior
* limiting participation in specific discussions
* removing harmful content
* restricting participation in project spaces

Actions should prioritize transparency and fairness.

---

# Final Principle

A universal symbol can only grow through cooperation.

The project welcomes disagreement, creativity, and improvement, but always within a foundation of respect and openness.

The symbol is open to everyone.

The community behind it should be open as well.
